Raja Club Athletic

The Green Eagles

Raja Casablanca is more than just a football club; it's a symbol of pride and passion for Moroccan football fans. Founded in 1949, Raja has become a powerhouse in African football, with a rich history of triumphs both domestically and internationally. Known for its fierce rivalry with Wydad Casablanca, Raja embodies the spirit of competition and the heart of Casablanca. The club's green and white colors represent the unwavering dedication and commitment of its supporters, who are a key part of its success. Raja is not just a team; it’s a family, united by the love of the game and the dream of glory

The fans of Raja Casablanca, known as the ‘Green Eagles,’ are the lifeblood of the club. Their unwavering loyalty, passion, and dedication make them one of the most passionate fanbases in African football. Whether at the stadium or supporting from afar, Raja’s fans are always there, creating an electrifying atmosphere that inspires the team. Their chants, colors, and energy represent a community bound together by a love for their club, always ready to stand behind their team through thick and thin. For Raja’s fans, it’s not just about football—it’s a way of life.
